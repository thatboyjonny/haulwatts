How we test

We own the fleet

A Hummer EV Omega Edition SUV, an F-150 Lightning Lariat Extended Range, a 2022 Model Y Performance, and a 2021 Model S refresh — bought, financed, insured, and driven daily. Written by an Air Force veteran and former Turo operator, not a press fleet. When something breaks or disappoints, you'll read about it.

We measure, not estimate

Range logs use metered energy at the wall and odometer miles, not the trip computer's optimism. Charging tests use timestamps and real statements. Every number on this site can be reproduced.

We review what we live with

Gear verdicts come from the equipment we already own and use daily. For everything else, we do research-driven comparisons and say so plainly. Some links earn us a commission — it never changes a verdict, and we disclose it on every page.
